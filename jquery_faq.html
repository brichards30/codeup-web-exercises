National Parks FAQs

Cane River Creole

  • Located in Natchitoches, LA
  • Oakland and Magnolia Plantations are two of the most intact Creole cotton plantations in the United States
  • Today, nearly 60 historic buildings of Oakland Plantation remain
  • Eight cabins of the Magnolia Plantation Quarters have survived.

Martin Luther King, Jr. National Historical Park

  • Located at his childhood home in Atlanta, GA
  • Includes locations where Dr. King was born, lived, worked, worshipped, and is buried.
  • Ebenezer Baptist Church, where Dr. King was baptized, was restored in 2001.
  • In March 1992, the Martin Luther King, Jr. "I Have a Dream" World Peace Rose Garden was planted.
